What am I looking at?

Four autonomous bots read live World Cup odds from TxODDS's TxLINE feed and place paper bets at real prices — shown above in plain English. They're graded by Closing Line Value (live) and settled P&L (when a match ends), and the strongest signals commit real USDT on-chain on Solana. The match theatre is a stylised view driven by real score/clock/odds/our-bets (TxLINE doesn't provide player tracking, so player positions are illustrative). Sections are tagged REAL vs SIMULATED.
